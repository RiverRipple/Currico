Why Currico?

During COVID, many students have found it has been extrememly difficult to participate in programs, internships, or even volunteer opportunities that they desire. With Currico, this will be an issue no more.

Our Mission

We believe all students should have the ability to take on new interests and challenges. Through our simple webpage, students will discover the inconviences from COVID will be a thing of the past, and their knowledge will pave the way for the future.
